Unlocking the Hidden Potential in Your Data

Spatial data can unlock hidden potential. By understanding "where" your data is at, new insights can be gathered to make informed decisions. If you already appreciate the power of GIS, we can provide advice in proposing workflows, or provide additional resources to complete efforts you have already developed.

Our Areas of Expertise

At Ceratopsian Consulting, we have experience in many different subspecialties regarding spatial data analysis. Areas of expertise include:

  • Creation of High Quality Paper Maps: Professional cartography for reports, presentations, and publications.
  • Field Data Collection: Mobile GIS solutions for gathering and validating spatial data in the field.
  • Database Curation: Organization, maintenance, and optimization of spatial databases.
  • Data Transformation/Automation: Streamlining workflows through custom tools and processes.
  • Web GIS: Interactive mapping applications and online spatial data platforms.
  • Digitization: Converting analog maps and information into digital geospatial data.
  • Raster Analysis: Advanced spatial modeling and analysis using raster datasets.
